About

Craft, code, and the pursuit of the extraordinary frame.

I'm Archie Bruce — an animator and computing student at the University of Sydney. My work lives at the intersection of hand-drawn tradition and contemporary technology: the warmth of pencil-on-paper and the precision of code.

Inspired by the discipline of Disney's Nine Old Men and the raw creative spirit of Robert Henri's Art Spirit, I believe every frame should be made with intention — whether it's drawn by hand or rendered by machine.

01

2D Animation

Traditional frame-by-frame and cut-out animation grounded in classical principles — squash, stretch, timing, and weight — applied with a contemporary eye.

02

Creative Technology

Building the tools animation needs: interpolation systems, AI-assisted workflows, and robotic drawing rigs that extend what the hand can do.

03

Direction & Story

Visual storytelling with a director's eye — developing concepts from initial sketch through to final composition with clarity and emotional intent.
